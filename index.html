Who am I?

a little bit about me... and my logo

Hi! I am John Carlo, you can call me "JC". I am a student visa holder taking an Advanced Diploma of Information Technology in Sydney. I have been working as a Quality Assurance Analyst for 2 years in the Philippines, before I moved to Sydney last December 2021. When I am free, I love to expand my software development and design knowledge by watching tutorials and taking courses online.

If you're keen to know me further, I have a link to my resume which you can access by clicking the button below.

Copy of JC's Resume

This logo came from the idea of my nickname — "JC". My sister used to call me "Jeez" just to shorten my name (kinda lazy I know right) so that she could only say 1 syllable. Short flashback, due to the Philippines' history being colonized once by the Spanish, we are using Spanish-derived counting systems. That "Jeez" sounds like "diez" in Spanish, or "dyis" in Tagalog, which is "ten" in English. Thus, by looking carefully at my logo it resembles the number 10 (might need to tilt your head at some angle though).

Please feel free to hover the logo to see what I am referring into.

I created this logo in my attempt to provide an identity for my freelance graphic designing job years back. However, due to my previous full-time work demands, I wasn't able to do it. Now that I have developed my first-ever portfolio website, which I haven't thought of before, it is my second chance to achieve it.
