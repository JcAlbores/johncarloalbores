Only If you're... intrigued

This logo of mine came from the idea of my nick name — "JC". My sister used to call me as "Jeez" just to shorten my name (kinda lazy I know right) so that she could only say 1 syllable. Short flashback, due to the Philippine's history being collonized once by Spanish, we are using Spanish-derived counting systems. That "Jeez" sounds like "diez" in Spanish, or "dyis" in Tagalog, which is "ten" in English. Thus, by looking carefully in my logo it resembles the number 10 (might need to tilt your head at some angle though).

I created this logo in my attempt to provide an identify for my freelance graphic designing job years back. However, due to my previous full-time work's demands, I wasn't able to do it. Now that I have developed my first ever portfolio website, which I haven't thought before, its my second chance to achieve it.
