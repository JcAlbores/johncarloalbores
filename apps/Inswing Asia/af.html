American Football


American football, also known as “gridiron”, is considered as the America’s greatest game and is the most popular sport in the United States. It is played by 11 players for both teams, which may freely substitute players between downs. Compared to other football codes, American football is a full-contact sport and is common for injuries, which is why players are highly encouraged to wear proper set of uniform and equipment.

American football related sports

1. Canadian football
Canadian football is the most dominant football form in Canada and it is closely related to American football. These two have some similar rules, however one of the differences of the two is the size of the Canadian football field (137m x 59m).

2. Arena football
One of the major variant of American football is the arena football. This sport is played by eight players for each team with an indoor rectangular field that measures 46 meter in length and 25.9 meters in width.

3. Flag football
Flag football is a non-contact variant of American football. This sport is less violent than the American football. This is played by trying to pull a flag tied around the ball carrier’s waist instead of forcing the ball carrier to fall to the ground.
