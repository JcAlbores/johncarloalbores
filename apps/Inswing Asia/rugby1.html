Equipment & Field


1. Rugby ball

The shape of a rugby ball must be prolate spheroid inflated with air. In rugby league, the size of the ball must be 11 inches long and 24 inches in circumference. On the other hand, rugby union ball must be 11 to 12 inches long and 23 to 24 in circumference.
2. Uniform and kit

Players must wear jersey, shorts, mouth guards, socks and other common wearables in football. They could also wear scrum caps, and shin guards to protect them from potential serious injuries.

3. Rugby Field

Rugby league is played in a field with 100m x 68 m dimensions, while in rugby union, 100m x 70m.
