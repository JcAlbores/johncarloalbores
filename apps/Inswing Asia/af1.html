Equipment & Field


1. The Football

The ball used in American football is inflated to 12 and a half to 13 and a half pounds per square inch and weighs for 397 to 425 grams. The ball has a long axis (11 to 11 and a half inches), a long circumference (28 to 28 and a half inches), and a short circumference (21 to 21 and a quarter). The dimensions of the football vary according to the variant of the game.

American football rectangular field has dimensions of 110 meters in length and 48.76 meters in width.
