What is Football?


Football is a popular team sports played by two teams and is commonly played in a rectangular field. For simpler definition, from its combined word "foot" and "ball", this sport is mostly played by passing the ball and scored by using the player's foot. The players' movements and rules of the game vary depending on the football code. Do not worry about the football code; it will be discussed on the next lessons.


Feel confused?


Note: You might be confused on the term "football" and might define this sport as the soccer, or rugby, or even the American football. In reality, the use of this term depends on the country. For example, Americans use this term for their American football, and not the soccer sport. These have always been confusing people on defining the football, in fact, these are football codes. Football has been developed in centuries, which resulted in different football varieties.


Football originated with laws, prohibiting the use of hands. But as generations pass by, new rules and regulations are implemented in the game. In shorter terms, football evolved and branched new variants.
