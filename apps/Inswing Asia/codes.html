Football Codes


Football Codes are also referred to as types of football. Due to the development of the sport through the generations, more variations, hybrid games, and recreations are developed. There are different football codes; there are Australian football, association football, Futsal, Gridiron football, Paralympic football (for disabled athletes), Gaelic football, Canadian football and many more. This lesson will focus on the world's most famous football codes: association football, rugby Football and American Football.
