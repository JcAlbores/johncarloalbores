Drills & Practices


Practice makes perfect. Drills, routines, exercises and practices are not only for the beginners, it is a continuous process that should be bounded a player even in his/her successful journey to the sport. Here are some drills that you can do to improve your skills in American and rugby football:

1. One-on-one blocking drill
This drill will aid the players to improve their footwork and hand technique. This will also improve the manipulation of a plater to a blocker with the use of their hands and upper body weight to get through the block.

2. Box drill
This practice will aid the players to condition their hitting and protecting, for offensive players, and proper tackling posture, for defensive players, in able to prevent serious injuries. This is done with four cones that is placed at the shape of a square.

3. Two-on-three passing drill
This drill will aid the players to learn and improve their passing routes, passing coverage skills, and catching the ball against the defenders. This will also improve the connection of the teammates for good passing and for maintaining the ball possession on the team.

4. Wind sprints
This practice will aid the players in developing their speed, stamina and lung capacity. This will also help the players in conditioning their body and losing fat, which are very essential for this sport.
